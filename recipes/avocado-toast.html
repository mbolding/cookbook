Avocado Toast

Prep: 5 mins Cook: 2 mins Serves: 1

Description

A simple yet satisfying breakfast or snack featuring creamy avocado on toasted sourdough bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 slice Sourdough bread
  • 1/2 ripe Avocado
  • 1 tsp Lemon juice
  • Pinch of Red pepper flakes
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Optional: 1 poached egg

Instructions

  1. Toast the sourdough bread to your desired level of crispness.
  2. In a small bowl, mash the avocado with lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Spread the mashed avocado evenly over the toast.
  4. Sprinkle with red pepper flakes and additional salt if needed.
  5. Top with a poached egg if desired and serve immediately.
