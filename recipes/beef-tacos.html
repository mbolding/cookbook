Street-Style Beef Tacos

Prep: 15 mins Cook: 20 mins Serves: 4

Description

Juicy, well-seasoned ground beef piled into warm corn tortillas and loaded with fresh toppings. Simple, satisfying, and always a crowd-pleaser. Set up a taco bar and let everyone build their own.

Ingredients

  • 500g lean ground beef
  • 1 small onion, finely diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/3 cup beef or chicken broth
  • 8 small corn or flour tortillas, warmed
  • Toppings: diced white onion, fresh cilantro, sliced jalapeño, lime wedges, salsa, sour cream

Instructions

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the ground beef and cook, breaking it apart, until browned, about 5–7 minutes. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add the diced onion to the beef and cook for 3 minutes until softened.
  3. Add garlic and all the spices. Cook for 1 minute, stirring constantly.
  4. Pour in the broth and stir to combine. Simmer for 3–5 minutes until the liquid is mostly absorbed. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Warm tortillas in a dry skillet or directly over a gas flame for 30 seconds per side.
  6. Spoon the beef into the tortillas and top with your choice of onion, cilantro, jalapeño, salsa, sour cream, and a squeeze of lime.
