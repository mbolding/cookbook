Blueberry Muffins

🫐
Prep: 15 mins Cook: 20 mins Makes: 12 muffins

Description

A classic and moist muffin filled with fresh blueberries, perfect for breakfast or a sweet snack.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups All-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup White sugar
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 2 tsp Baking powder
  • 1/3 cup Vegetable oil
  • 1 Egg
  • 1/3 cup Milk
  • 1 cup Fresh blueberries

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F). Grease muffin cups or use liners.
  2. Combine flour, sugar, salt, and baking powder in a large bowl.
  3. Place vegetable oil into a 1-cup measuring cup; add the egg and enough milk to fill the cup. Mix with flour mixture.
  4. Fold in blueberries.
  5. Fill muffin cups right to the top, and bake for 20-25 minutes in the preheated oven.
  6. Let cool in the pan for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack.
