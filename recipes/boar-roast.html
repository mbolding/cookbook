Mountain Boar Roast

🍖
Prep: 20 mins Cook: 3 hours Serves: 8 Dwarves

Description

A massive, salt-crusted boar roast with skin that shatters like glass. A staple of any feast in the Great Hall of Thráin.

Ingredients

  • 3kg Pork shoulder or wild boar leg (bone-in, skin-on)
  • 3 tbsp Coarse sea salt
  • 1 tbsp Black peppercorns, crushed
  • 4 cloves Garlic, smashed
  • 2 sprigs Rosemary
  • 1 cup Dark ale (for the pan)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C) for the initial sear.
  2. Score the skin in a diamond pattern, being careful not to cut into the meat.
  3. Rub the salt and pepper deep into the scores.
  4. Place garlic and rosemary in a roasting pan. Set the meat on top.
  5. Roast at 450°F for 30 minutes to start the crackling.
  6. Reduce heat to 325°F (160°C). Pour the ale into the pan (not over the skin!).
  7. Roast for another 2.5 hours until the meat is fork-tender.
  8. Let rest for 20 minutes. Carve into thick slabs.
