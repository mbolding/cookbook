Chicken Caesar Salad

Prep: 15 mins Cook: 10 mins Serves: 2

Description

A hearty and classic salad with crisp romaine, grilled chicken, savory croutons, and creamy Caesar dressing.

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken breasts, grilled and sliced
  • 1 head Romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/2 cup Croutons
  • 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese, shaved
  • 1/4 cup Caesar dressing
  • 1 tsp Lemon juice
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Wash and chop the romaine lettuce and place it in a large salad bowl.
  2. Add the sliced grilled chicken and crunchy croutons.
  3. Drizzle the Caesar dressing over the salad and add lemon juice.
  4. Toss the salad gently to coat the lettuce and chicken evenly.
  5. Top with shaved Parmesan cheese and freshly ground black pepper.
  6. Serve immediately as a light and refreshing main dish.
