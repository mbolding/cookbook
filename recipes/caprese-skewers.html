Caprese Skewers

Prep: 15 mins Cook: 0 mins Makes: 20 skewers

Description

A fun and colorful appetizer that highlights the classic combination of fresh mozzarella, tomatoes, and basil.

Ingredients

  • 20 Cherry tomatoes
  • 20 Mini mozzarella balls (bocconcini)
  • 20 Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp Balsamic glaze
  • 1 tbsp Extra virgin olive oil
  • Wooden skewers
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Thread a cherry tomato, a folded basil leaf, and a mozzarella ball onto each wooden skewer.
  2. Arrange the skewers on a serving platter.
  3. Drizzle with extra virgin olive oil and balsamic glaze.
  4. Sprinkle with salt and freshly ground black pepper.
  5. Serve immediately at room temperature.
