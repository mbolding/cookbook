Classic Cheesecake

🍰
Prep: 20 mins Cook: 1 hr 10 mins + chill Serves: 12

Description

Creamy, dense, and perfectly tangy. A New York-style cheesecake with a buttery graham cracker crust that sets beautifully overnight. Top with fresh berries or cherry compote for a showstopping dessert.

Ingredients

  • Crust: 200g graham crackers, crushed; 80g melted butter; 2 tbsp sugar
  • Filling: 900g cream cheese, softened; 1 cup sugar; 3 tbsp cornstarch; 3 large eggs; 1 egg yolk; 1 cup sour cream; 2 tsp vanilla extract; zest of 1 lemon
  • Fresh berries or cherry compote, to serve

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (160°C). Mix crushed crackers, melted butter, and 2 tbsp sugar. Press firmly into the base of a 9-inch springform pan. Bake for 10 minutes. Cool.
  2. Beat cream cheese until completely smooth. Add sugar and cornstarch; beat until combined.
  3. Add eggs and yolk one at a time, beating on low after each addition. Do not over-mix.
  4. Fold in sour cream, vanilla, and lemon zest until just combined.
  5. Pour filling over the crust. Place the pan in a large roasting dish and fill with 1 inch of hot water (water bath prevents cracking).
  6. Bake for 60–70 minutes until the edges are set but the center still wobbles slightly.
  7. Turn off the oven, crack the door, and let the cheesecake cool inside for 1 hour. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight before serving.
