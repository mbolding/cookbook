Chicken Caesar Wrap

Prep: 15 mins Cook: 15 mins Serves: 2

Description

Everything you love about a Caesar salad, wrapped up and ready to go. Crispy grilled chicken, crunchy romaine, shaved Parmesan, and creamy Caesar dressing in a toasted flour tortilla.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless chicken breasts
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 large flour tortillas
  • 3 cups romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/4 cup shaved Parmesan
  • 1/2 cup croutons, lightly crushed
  • 4 tbsp Caesar dressing
  • 1 lemon, for squeezing

Instructions

  1. Season chicken with garlic powder, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high. Cook chicken for 6–7 minutes per side until golden and cooked through. Rest for 5 minutes, then slice thinly.
  3. Warm tortillas in a dry pan for 30 seconds each side.
  4. Toss romaine with Caesar dressing and a squeeze of lemon.
  5. Layer the dressed lettuce, sliced chicken, Parmesan, and crushed croutons down the center of each tortilla.
  6. Fold in the sides, then roll tightly. Slice in half diagonally and serve immediately.
