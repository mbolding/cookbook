Chicken Tikka Masala

Prep: 20 mins Cook: 40 mins Serves: 4

Description

Tender chunks of marinated chicken simmered in a rich, creamy tomato-spiced sauce. This restaurant-favorite is surprisingly achievable at home and pairs beautifully with basmati rice or naan.

Ingredients

  • 600g boneless chicken thighs, cut into chunks
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 large onion, finely diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, to garnish

Instructions

  1. Combine yogurt, lemon juice, and half the spices. Add chicken chunks, toss to coat, and marinate for at least 1 hour (overnight is best).
  2. Cook the marinated chicken in a hot skillet or under a broiler until charred at the edges, about 5–6 minutes per side. Set aside.
  3. Melt butter in a large pan over medium heat. Sauté onion until golden, about 8 minutes.
  4. Add garlic and ginger; cook for 2 minutes until fragrant.
  5. Stir in remaining spices and cook for 1 minute.
  6. Add crushed tomatoes and simmer for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. Stir in the cream and add the cooked chicken. Simmer for another 10 minutes.
  8. Season with salt, garnish with cilantro, and serve with rice or naan.
