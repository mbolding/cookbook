Cinnamon Rolls

🧁
Prep: 30 mins + rise Cook: 25 mins Serves: 12

Description

Soft, pillowy rolls swirled with cinnamon-brown sugar butter and dripping with cream cheese icing. Worth every minute of patience — the smell of these baking will fill your entire home.

Ingredients

  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 packet (2 1/4 tsp) instant yeast
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1 large egg
  • Filling: 1/3 cup softened butter, 3/4 cup brown sugar, 2 tbsp cinnamon
  • Icing: 4 oz cream cheese, 1 cup powdered sugar, 2 tbsp milk, 1 tsp vanilla

Instructions

  1. Combine flour, sugar, yeast, and salt. Add warm milk, melted butter, and egg. Mix until a soft dough forms, then knead for 5–7 minutes.
  2. Cover and let rise in a warm spot for 1 hour until doubled in size.
  3. Roll the dough into a large rectangle (about 16×12 inches). Spread softened butter evenly, then sprinkle with brown sugar and cinnamon.
  4. Roll tightly from the long side into a log. Cut into 12 even slices.
  5. Place rolls in a greased 9×13 baking dish. Cover and let rise for another 30 minutes.
  6. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Bake for 22–25 minutes until golden.
  7. While rolls bake, beat cream cheese, powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla until smooth. Spread icing generously over the warm rolls and serve.
