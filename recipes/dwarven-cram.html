Dwarven Cram

⛏️
Prep: 10 mins Cook: 40 mins More of a "chewing exercise" than a meal

Description

A hard, dense, and incredibly durable biscuit used by the Dwarves of the Iron Hills and Erebor for long subterranean journeys. It isn't as pleasant as Lembas, but it gets the job done.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups Stone-ground whole wheat flour
  • 1 cup Rolled oats, pulsed into a coarse flour
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1/4 cup Honey (for a hint of flavor)
  • 1 cup Water (add slowly)
  • 2 tbsp Lard or butter (melted)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (160°C).
  2. Mix the flour, oat flour, and salt in a heavy iron or stone bowl.
  3. Combine honey, melted fat, and half the water. Pour into the flour.
  4. Mix by hand, adding water until a very stiff, dry dough forms. It should be difficult to knead.
  5. Roll out to 1-inch thickness and cut into thick rectangles.
  6. Prick the surface deeply with a fork multiple times to ensure it dries out completely.
  7. Bake for 40-50 minutes until rock-hard and slightly browned.
  8. Store in a linen bag. It will last as long as the mountains themselves.
