Classic Greek Salad

Prep: 15 mins Cook: 0 mins Serves: 2

Description

A fresh, crisp salad that brings together the classic Mediterranean flavors of feta, olives, and cucumbers.

Ingredients

  • 2 large Tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 Cucumber, sliced
  • 1/2 Red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives
  • 200g Feta cheese, cubed or crumbled
  • 3 tbsp Extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp Dried oregano
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion.
  2. Add the Kalamata olives and feta cheese.
  3. Drizzle with extra virgin olive oil.
  4. Sprinkle dried oregano, salt, and black pepper over the salad.
  5. Toss gently to combine all ingredients without breaking the feta.
  6. Serve fresh, ideally with a side of crusty bread.
