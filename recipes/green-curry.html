Thai Green Curry

Prep: 15 mins Cook: 20 mins Serves: 4

Description

A fragrant and spicy Thai classic. Tender chicken and vegetables simmered in a creamy coconut milk sauce infused with green chili, lemongrass, and Thai basil.

Ingredients

  • 500g Chicken breast, sliced
  • 3 tbsp Thai green curry paste
  • 400ml Coconut milk (full fat)
  • 1 cup Bamboo shoots or snap peas
  • 1 Red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 tbsp Fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp Brown sugar
  • Handful of Thai basil leaves

Instructions

  1. Heat a splash of oil in a large pan or wok. Add curry paste and fry for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  2. Pour in 100ml of coconut milk and stir to combine with the paste.
  3. Add the chicken and cook until no longer pink on the outside.
  4. Stir in the remaining coconut milk, fish sauce, and sugar. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Add the vegetables and cook for another 3-5 minutes until tender but still crisp.
  6. Turn off the heat and stir in the Thai basil leaves.
  7. Serve hot with jasmine rice.
