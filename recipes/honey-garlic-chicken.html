Honey Garlic Chicken

Prep: 10 mins Cook: 25 mins Serves: 4

Description

Sticky, glossy, and irresistibly sweet-savory. Pan-seared chicken thighs glazed in a luscious honey garlic sauce that caramelizes beautifully. A guaranteed family favorite that pairs perfectly with steamed rice.

Ingredients

  • 8 boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1/3 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch mixed with 2 tbsp water
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp sriracha (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Sesame seeds and sliced scallions, to garnish

Instructions

  1. Season chicken thighs with salt and pepper on both sides.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Sear chicken for 4–5 minutes per side until golden. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, reduce heat to medium. Add garlic and sauté for 30 seconds.
  4. Whisk together honey, soy sauce, rice vinegar, and sriracha (if using). Pour into the pan and bring to a simmer.
  5. Stir in the cornstarch slurry and cook until the sauce thickens, about 1–2 minutes.
  6. Return the chicken to the pan and coat in the glaze. Simmer for 3–4 minutes until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce is sticky.
  7. Garnish with sesame seeds and scallions. Serve over rice.
