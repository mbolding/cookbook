Spinach & Ricotta Lasagna

Prep: 30 mins Cook: 45 mins Serves: 6

Description

A hearty vegetarian alternative to the classic meat lasagna. Layers of tender pasta, creamy ricotta, fresh spinach, and rich marinara sauce.

Ingredients

  • 12 Lasagna noodles
  • 500g Ricotta cheese
  • 300g Fresh spinach, wilted and squeezed dry
  • 700ml Marinara sauce
  • 2 cups Shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup Grated Parmesan
  • 1 Egg, lightly beaten
  • 1 tsp Dried oregano

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions.
  2. In a bowl, mix ricotta, wilted spinach, egg, oregano, and half the Parmesan.
  3. Spread a thin layer of marinara sauce in a 9x13 baking dish.
  4. Layer 3-4 noodles, followed by a layer of the ricotta mixture, marinara, and mozzarella.
  5. Repeat layers, ending with marinara and a generous topping of mozzarella and Parmesan.
  6. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 20 minutes until bubbly.
  7. Let rest for 10 minutes before slicing.
