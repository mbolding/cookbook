Lembas Bread

🍃
Prep: 15 mins Cook: 20 mins One bite sustains a grown man for a day

Description

The precious waybread of the Eldar. Crisp, golden, and slightly sweet, it stays fresh for many weeks when wrapped in its traditional mallorn leaves.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups All-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp Baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 cup Cold butter, cubed
  • 1/3 cup Brown sugar
  • 1 tsp Cinnamon
  • 2/3 cup Heavy cream
  • 1/2 tsp Vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp Honey

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Cut in the cold butter with a fork or pastry cutter until the mixture resembles fine crumbs.
  4. Stir in the sugar and cinnamon.
  5. Add the cream, vanilla, and honey. Mix until a stiff dough forms.
  6. Roll out the dough to 1/2-inch thickness. Cut into 3-inch squares.
  7. Use a knife to score an "X" across the top of each square (do not cut all the way through).
  8. Bake for 12-15 minutes until lightly golden. Let cool completely.
