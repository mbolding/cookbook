Baked Lemon Salmon

Prep: 10 mins Cook: 15 mins Serves: 2

Description

A quick and healthy way to prepare salmon, featuring fresh lemon and herbs for a bright, clean flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 Salmon fillets
  • 1 Lemon, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp Olive oil
  • 2 cloves Garlic, minced
  • Fresh dill, chopped
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. Place salmon fillets on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, minced garlic, salt, and pepper.
  4. Brush the mixture over the salmon fillets.
  5. Place lemon slices on top of each fillet.
  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes until the salmon is cooked through and easily flakes with a fork.
  7. Garnish with fresh dill before serving.
