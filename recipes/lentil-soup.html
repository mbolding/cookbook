Lentil & Spinach Soup

Prep: 10 mins Cook: 35 mins Serves: 4

Description

A hearty and nutritious soup filled with fiber-rich lentils, fresh spinach, and aromatic spices.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Brown or green lentils, rinsed
  • 1 large Onion, chopped
  • 2 Carrots, diced
  • 2 cups Fresh spinach
  • 1 can Diced tomatoes (400g)
  • 4 cups Vegetable broth
  • 1 tsp Cumin
  • 1 tsp Turmeric
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat a large pot over medium heat with a splash of oil. Sauté onion and carrots for 5 minutes.
  2. Stir in cumin and turmeric and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Add lentils, diced tomatoes, and vegetable broth.
  4. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 25-30 minutes until lentils are tender.
  5. Stir in fresh spinach and cook until wilted (about 2 minutes).
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve warm with a squeeze of fresh lemon juice if desired.
