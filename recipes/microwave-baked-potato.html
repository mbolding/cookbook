Microwave Baked Potato

🥔
Prep: 2 mins Cook: 8 mins Serves: 1

Description

A fluffy, steaming-hot baked potato in under ten minutes — no oven preheating required. Load it up with butter, sour cream, cheese, and chives for the ultimate easy comfort meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 large russet potato
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • Salt
  • Toppings: butter, sour cream, shredded cheddar, chives, bacon bits, salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Scrub the potato clean and pat dry. Poke it all over with a fork (about 8–10 times) to let steam escape — this prevents it from exploding.
  2. Rub the skin lightly with olive oil and sprinkle with salt.
  3. Place on a microwave-safe plate. Microwave on high for 5 minutes.
  4. Carefully flip the potato and microwave for another 3–5 minutes until a fork slides in easily to the center.
  5. Let it rest for 2 minutes. Cut a slit down the top and squeeze the sides to open it up.
  6. Fill with butter, sour cream, cheese, chives, and bacon bits. Season and serve hot.
