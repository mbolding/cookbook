Microwave Mac & Cheese

Prep: 2 mins Cook: 5 mins Serves: 1

Description

Creamy, cheesy, single-serve mac and cheese made entirely in the microwave — no stove, no draining, no boxed mix. Just real cheese melted into perfectly cooked pasta. College dorm essential, gourmet results.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup elbow macaroni (dry)
  • 1/2 cup water, plus more as needed
  • 1/4 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of paprika or mustard powder (optional)

Instructions

  1. Add macaroni and water to a large microwave-safe bowl (use bigger than you think — it bubbles up). The water should cover the pasta by about an inch.
  2. Microwave on high for 2 minutes. Stir. Microwave for 2 more minutes. Stir again. If the pasta isn't tender, add a splash more water and microwave for another 1 minute.
  3. Drain any excess water. While the pasta is still hot, add butter, milk, and shredded cheese.
  4. Stir vigorously until the cheese melts into a creamy sauce. The residual heat from the pasta does the work.
  5. Season with salt and a pinch of paprika or mustard powder if you like. Eat immediately.
