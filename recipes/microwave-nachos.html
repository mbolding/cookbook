Microwave Loaded Nachos

🌮
Prep: 5 mins Cook: 2 mins Serves: 2

Description

Crunchy tortilla chips buried under melted cheese, jalapeños, and all the fixings — assembled and melted in the microwave in minutes. The ultimate lazy snack done right.

Ingredients

  • Large handful of tortilla chips
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar or Mexican blend cheese
  • 1/4 cup canned black beans, drained
  • 2 tbsp pickled jalapeño slices
  • Toppings (after microwaving): sour cream, guacamole, salsa, diced tomato, sliced scallions, cilantro

Instructions

  1. Spread tortilla chips in a single layer on a microwave-safe plate.
  2. Scatter black beans and jalapeño slices over the chips. Top generously with shredded cheese.
  3. Microwave on high for 45 seconds to 1 minute until the cheese is fully melted and bubbly. For extra chips, repeat with a second layer and microwave again.
  4. Remove carefully (the plate will be hot). Immediately top with sour cream, guacamole, salsa, diced tomato, scallions, and cilantro.
  5. Eat right away while the chips are still crunchy under the cheese. Nachos wait for no one.
