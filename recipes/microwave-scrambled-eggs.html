Microwave Scrambled Eggs

Prep: 1 min Cook: 2 mins Serves: 1

Description

Impossibly fluffy scrambled eggs in under three minutes with zero pans to wash. The secret is short bursts with stirring in between — you'll get soft, pillowy curds every single time.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Shredded cheese, chives, or hot sauce (optional toppings)

Instructions

  1. Add butter to a microwave-safe mug or bowl. Microwave for 15–20 seconds until melted.
  2. Crack in the eggs, add milk, salt, and pepper. Whisk well with a fork until the yolks and whites are fully combined.
  3. Microwave on high for 30 seconds. Remove and stir with a fork, pushing the set edges toward the center.
  4. Microwave for another 30 seconds. Stir again.
  5. Microwave for a final 15–30 seconds until eggs are just set but still look slightly wet — they'll continue cooking from residual heat.
  6. Top with cheese, chives, or hot sauce as desired. Serve immediately.
