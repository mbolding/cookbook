Miruvor (Elven Cordial)

Prep: 5 mins Steep: 2 hours Restorative

Description

A revitalizing, honeyed cordial made by the Elves of Rivendell. It is said to give renewed strength to those whose hearts are weary.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Filtered water
  • 1/4 cup Wildflower honey
  • 1 tbsp Dried elderflower
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2-3 Thin slices of fresh ginger
  • 1 strip of Lemon zest
  • Splash of rosewater (optional)

Instructions

  1. Bring the water to a simmer in a small saucepan.
  2. Stir in the honey until completely dissolved.
  3. Add the elderflower, cinnamon stick, ginger, and lemon zest.
  4. Remove from heat, cover, and let steep for at least 2 hours (or overnight for a stronger infusion).
  5. Strain through a fine-mesh sieve or cheesecloth.
  6. Stir in the rosewater if using.
  7. Serve at room temperature in small silver cups or crystal glasses.
