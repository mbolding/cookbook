Miso Soup

🥣
Prep: 5 mins Cook: 10 mins Serves: 4

Description

A gentle, nourishing Japanese classic. Silky dashi broth enriched with umami-rich miso paste, studded with soft tofu cubes, wakame seaweed, and sliced scallions. Comforting in its simplicity.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups dashi stock (or water with 1 tsp dashi powder)
  • 3 tbsp white or yellow miso paste
  • 150g silken tofu, cubed
  • 2 tbsp dried wakame seaweed
  • 2 scallions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tsp soy sauce (optional)

Instructions

  1. Soak the dried wakame in warm water for 5 minutes until rehydrated. Drain and set aside.
  2. Bring the dashi stock to a gentle simmer in a saucepan. Do not boil vigorously.
  3. Add the tofu cubes and wakame. Simmer gently for 2 minutes.
  4. Remove from heat. Place miso paste in a small strainer or ladle, submerge it in the broth, and stir to dissolve. Never boil miso — it destroys the delicate flavor.
  5. Add soy sauce if desired. Ladle into bowls and garnish with sliced scallions.
