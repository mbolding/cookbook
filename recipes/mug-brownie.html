Microwave Mug Brownie

Prep: 3 mins Cook: 70 secs Serves: 1

Description

Dense, fudgy, and intensely chocolatey — a single-serve brownie that's ready in under five minutes. The inside stays gloriously gooey while the top forms a crinkly crust. Instant chocolate therapy.

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp melted butter
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp chocolate chips
  • A scoop of vanilla ice cream, to serve

Instructions

  1. In a microwave-safe mug, stir together melted butter, sugar, and brown sugar.
  2. Add cocoa powder, salt, and vanilla. Mix until smooth.
  3. Stir in the egg yolk, then fold in the flour until just combined — don't overmix. Scatter chocolate chips on top.
  4. Microwave on high for 60–70 seconds. The brownie should be set around the edges but look slightly underdone in the center — it firms up as it cools.
  5. Let it rest for 1 minute. Top with a scoop of vanilla ice cream and eat straight from the mug.
