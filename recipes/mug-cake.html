Microwave Mug Cake

Prep: 3 mins Cook: 90 secs Serves: 1

Description

A warm, fluffy chocolate cake made entirely in a mug and ready in under five minutes. Dangerously easy and perfectly portioned for one — top with a scoop of ice cream for an instant upgrade.

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • 3 tbsp milk
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil or melted butter
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp chocolate chips (optional)
  • Ice cream or whipped cream, to serve

Instructions

  1. In a large microwave-safe mug, whisk together flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt with a fork.
  2. Add milk, oil, and vanilla extract. Stir until smooth and no lumps remain.
  3. Fold in chocolate chips if using.
  4. Microwave on high for 60–90 seconds. The cake should be set on top but still slightly gooey in the center.
  5. Let it rest for 30 seconds (it's extremely hot inside). Top with ice cream or whipped cream and enjoy straight from the mug.
