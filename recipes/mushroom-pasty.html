Moria Mushroom Pasty

🥧
Prep: 40 mins Cook: 30 mins Makes 6 Pasties

Description

Hand-held pies filled with deep-cave mushrooms, onions, and a splash of stout. Designed to be eaten with one hand while holding a pickaxe in the other.

Ingredients

  • 500g Shortcrust pastry (pre-made or handmade)
  • 400g Mixed mushrooms (Chestnut, Portobello), roughly chopped
  • 1 Large onion, diced
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 1 tbsp Flour
  • 1/4 cup Stout or dark beer
  • 1 tsp Thyme
  • 1 Egg (for wash)

Instructions

  1. Sauté onions and mushrooms in butter until the moisture has evaporated and they are golden.
  2. Stir in flour, then add the stout. Simmer until the liquid forms a thick gravy. Add thyme, salt, and pepper. Let cool.
  3. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  4. Roll out pastry and cut into 6-inch circles.
  5. Place a large spoonful of the filling on one half of each circle.
  6. Fold over, crimp the edges tightly with a fork, and brush with beaten egg.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes until golden brown and sturdy.
