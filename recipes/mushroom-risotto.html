Mushroom Risotto

Prep: 10 mins Cook: 30 mins Serves: 4

Description

A classic Italian dish that is rich, creamy, and packed with earthy mushroom flavor. Worth every stir!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups Arborio rice
  • 500g Mixed mushrooms, sliced
  • 1.5 liters Vegetable broth, warm
  • 1/2 cup White wine
  • 1 Onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh thyme and parsley

Instructions

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium heat with 1 tbsp butter. Sauté mushrooms until browned and set aside.
  2. In the same skillet, melt the remaining butter and sauté onion and garlic until softened.
  3. Add Arborio rice and toast for 2 minutes, stirring constantly.
  4. Deglaze the pan with white wine and cook until absorbed.
  5. Add the warm broth one ladle at a time, stirring frequently and waiting for it to be absorbed before adding more.
  6. Continue until the rice is tender but firm (al dente). This takes about 20 minutes.
  7. Stir in the sautéed mushrooms, Parmesan cheese, and fresh herbs.
  8. Serve hot with an extra sprinkle of Parmesan.
