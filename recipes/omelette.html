Simple Omelette

Prep: 5 mins Cook: 5 mins Serves: 1

Description

A fluffy and classic omelette that is perfect for breakfast or a quick lunch. Customize it with your favorite fillings.

Ingredients

  • 2 large Eggs
  • 1 tbsp Milk
  • 1 tbsp Butter
  • 1/4 cup Grated cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Chopped chives or parsley (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk the eggs and milk together until well combined. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Melt the butter in a small non-stick skillet over medium-low heat.
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the skillet and let it cook for a minute without stirring.
  4. Gently push the cooked edges towards the center, allowing the uncooked egg to flow to the edges.
  5. Once the eggs are almost set but still slightly moist on top, sprinkle the cheese over one half.
  6. Fold the omelette in half and cook for another 30-60 seconds until the cheese is melted.
  7. Slide the omelette onto a plate and garnish with fresh herbs.
