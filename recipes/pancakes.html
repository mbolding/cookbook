Fluffy Pancakes

Prep: 5 mins Cook: 20 mins Serves: 2

Description

Thick, golden, and impossibly fluffy pancakes made from scratch. The secret is not overmixing the batter — a few lumps are a good thing. Serve stacked high with maple syrup and butter.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup buttermilk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter, plus more for cooking
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Maple syrup, to serve

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Do not overmix — lumps are okay.
  4. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Add a small knob of butter.
  5. Pour about 1/4 cup of batter per pancake onto the skillet.
  6. Cook until bubbles form on the surface and the edges look set, about 2–3 minutes. Flip and cook for another 1–2 minutes.
  7. Serve immediately with maple syrup and butter.
