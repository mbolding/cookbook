Basil Pesto Pasta

🌿
Prep: 10 mins Cook: 10 mins Serves: 4

Description

Vibrant, herby, and impossibly fresh. Homemade basil pesto tossed with hot pasta and finished with toasted pine nuts and a shower of Parmesan. Summer in a bowl — no cooking the sauce required.

Ingredients

  • 400g fusilli or penne
  • 2 packed cups fresh basil leaves
  • 1/3 cup pine nuts, lightly toasted
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese, plus extra to serve
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved (optional garnish)

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water before draining.
  2. While the pasta cooks, combine basil, pine nuts, garlic, and Parmesan in a food processor. Pulse until coarsely chopped.
  3. With the processor running, drizzle in the olive oil in a steady stream until smooth. Add lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Pulse to combine.
  4. Toss the hot drained pasta with the pesto, adding reserved pasta water a splash at a time until the sauce coats every piece.
  5. Serve with extra Parmesan, a few toasted pine nuts, and cherry tomatoes if desired.
