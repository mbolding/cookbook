Honey-Thyme Rainbow Trout

🐟
Prep: 10 mins Cook: 12 mins Serves: 2

Description

Fresh-caught stream trout, roasted with wild herbs and a touch of honey. Simple, elegant, and perfectly balanced.

Ingredients

  • 2 Whole rainbow trout, cleaned
  • 2 tbsp Wildflower honey
  • 1 tbsp Olive oil
  • 4 sprigs Fresh thyme
  • 1 Lemon, thinly sliced
  • Sea salt and cracked black pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a tray with parchment.
  2. Pat the trout dry with paper towels.
  3. Whisk honey and oil together. Brush over the inside and outside of the fish.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Stuff the cavity of each fish with lemon slices and thyme sprigs.
  6. Roast for 10-12 minutes until the flesh flakes easily with a fork.
  7. Serve immediately with a side of woodland greens.
