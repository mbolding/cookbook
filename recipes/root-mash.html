Stone-Hearth Root Mash

🥔
Prep: 15 mins Cook: 25 mins Hearty & Filling

Description

A rugged, textured mash of earth-grown vegetables. No fancy purees here—this is a thick, buttery side fit for a mountain king.

Ingredients

  • 3 Large potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 2 Large parsnips, peeled and cubed
  • 1 Large turnip, peeled and cubed
  • 100g Butter (the more the better)
  • 1/4 cup Heavy cream or whole milk
  • Salt and a lot of black pepper

Instructions

  1. Boil the potatoes, parsnips, and turnip in salted water until soft (about 20 minutes).
  2. Drain thoroughly and let sit for a minute to allow steam to escape (this keeps it from being watery).
  3. Add the butter and cream.
  4. Mash roughly with a heavy-duty masher. Leave some chunks for texture.
  5. Season generously with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve in a stone bowl alongside a heavy roast.
