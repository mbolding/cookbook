Smoked Salmon Bagel

Prep: 10 mins No Cook Serves: 1

Description

The ultimate deli classic. A toasted bagel spread with whipped cream cheese and topped with silky smoked salmon, sharp red onion, and salty capers.

Ingredients

  • 1 Everything bagel, halved
  • 2 tbsp Cream cheese
  • 50g Smoked salmon
  • 1/4 Red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tsp Capers, drained
  • Fresh dill and a squeeze of lemon
  • Black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Toast the bagel halves until golden brown.
  2. Generously spread cream cheese on both halves.
  3. Layer the smoked salmon on the bottom half.
  4. Top with red onion slices, capers, and fresh dill.
  5. Add a squeeze of lemon juice and a crack of black pepper.
  6. Close the bagel or enjoy open-faced.
