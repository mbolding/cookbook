Sticky Toffee Pudding

Prep: 20 mins Cook: 35 mins Serves: 6

Description

A classic British comfort dessert. A moist date cake smothered in a rich, buttery toffee sauce. Best served warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

Ingredients

  • 175g Dates, chopped
  • 175ml Boiling water
  • 1 tsp Baking soda
  • 75g Butter, softened
  • 150g Brown sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 175g Self-raising flour
  • 1 tsp Vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Soak chopped dates in boiling water with baking soda for 10 minutes. Mash slightly.
  2. Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C). Grease a baking dish.
  3. Cream butter and sugar together, then beat in eggs one at a time.
  4. Gently fold in the flour, vanilla, and the date mixture (including the soaking water).
  5. Pour into the baking dish and bake for 30-35 minutes until springy to the touch.
  6. While baking, make toffee sauce by simmering butter, sugar, and cream until thickened.
  7. Pour some sauce over the warm cake and serve the rest on the side.
