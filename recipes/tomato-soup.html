Roasted Tomato Soup

Prep: 15 mins Cook: 45 mins Serves: 4

Description

A rich, creamy soup made from slow-roasted tomatoes, garlic, and onions. Perfect with a grilled cheese sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 1.5kg Roma tomatoes, halved
  • 1 large Onion, quartered
  • 1 head Garlic, top sliced off
  • 2 tbsp Olive oil
  • 500ml Vegetable broth
  • 1/4 cup Fresh basil leaves
  • 1/2 cup Heavy cream (optional)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. Place tomatoes, onion, and garlic on a baking sheet. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Roast for 40-45 minutes until charred and softened.
  4. Squeeze the roasted garlic cloves out of their skin into a blender.
  5. Add the roasted tomatoes, onion, and fresh basil to the blender. Blend until smooth.
  6. Transfer the mixture to a pot, add vegetable broth, and simmer for 10 minutes.
  7. Stir in heavy cream if using, and serve warm.
