Spicy Tuna Roll

Prep: 30 mins Cook: 15 mins Serves: 2

Description

A simple and tasty sushi roll with fresh tuna and a spicy mayo kick, perfect for sushi lovers at home.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Sushi rice, cooked
  • 1 tbsp Rice vinegar
  • 200g Sashimi-grade tuna, cubed
  • 1 tbsp Sriracha sauce
  • 1 tbsp Japanese mayo
  • 4 Nori sheets (seaweed)
  • Cucumber slices, thinly sliced
  • Sesame seeds

Instructions

  1. Mix the cooked sushi rice with rice vinegar and let it cool.
  2. In a small bowl, combine cubed tuna with Sriracha and mayo to make the spicy tuna filling.
  3. Place a nori sheet on a bamboo sushi mat.
  4. Spread a thin layer of sushi rice over the nori, leaving about 1 inch at the top.
  5. Arrange cucumber slices and spicy tuna filling in a line across the center of the rice.
  6. Carefully roll the sushi mat away from you, pressing gently to form a tight roll.
  7. Use a sharp, damp knife to cut the roll into 6-8 pieces.
  8. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and serve with soy sauce and wasabi.
