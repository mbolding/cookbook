Wild Woodland Salad

Prep: 15 mins No Cook Serves: 2

Description

A fresh, bright salad inspired by the forests of Lothlórien. Features bitter greens, sweet berries, and earthy nuts.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups Mixed greens (arugula, spinach, watercress)
  • 1/2 cup Fresh blackberries or blueberries
  • 1/4 cup Toasted pine nuts
  • 50g Creamy goat cheese, crumbled
  • Edible flower petals (violets or nasturtiums)
  • Dressing: 2 tbsp olive oil, 1 tbsp blackberry vinegar, 1 tsp honey, salt/pepper.

Instructions

  1. Wash and dry the mixed greens thoroughly.
  2. In a small jar, whisk together the oil, vinegar, honey, and seasoning.
  3. Toss the greens lightly with the dressing in a large wooden bowl.
  4. Top with the fresh berries, toasted pine nuts, and goat cheese.
  5. Scatter the edible flower petals over the top just before serving.
